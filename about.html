What is Hack Club?

Hack Club is a global, non-profit network of student-led coding clubs designed for high schoolers and teenagers.

Key Things You Need To Know:

    LEARNING BY MAKING:

    No boring lecturers. you learn programming by actually building games, apps, and websites from day one.b

    FREE PREMIUM RESOURCES:

    Members get free (.com) domains, Zoom accounts, and access to hardware grants.

    GLOBAL COMMUNITY:

    You join a massive, supportive network of thousands of young makers worldwide on Discord to share projects and collaborate.
