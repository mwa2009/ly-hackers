Hack club launches in libya!

libya's tech community just took a massive leap forward with the official arrival of Hack club.

what is Hack Club?

It's a global, non-profit network of student-led coding clubs designed for high schoolers and teenagers.

Key Things You Need To Know:

*LEARNING BY MAKING: no boring lecturers. you learn programming by actually building games,apps,and websites from day one.

*FREE PREMIUM RESOURCES: members get free (.com) domains,zoom accounts,and access to hardware grants.

*GLOBAL COMMUNITY you join a massive, supportive network of thousands of young makers worldwide on discord to share projects and collaborate.
